The Hills Keeps Going and Going and...

Wed., Jul. 23, 2008 12:51 PM PDT by Marc Malkin

DNKY

Get ready for an extra two hours of The Hills—even before the fourth season premieres next month.

MTV is currently putting together a two-hour special about the best on-camera moments from Lauren Conrad & Co.

"All the girls are being interviewed for it, and celebrities are going to give commentary," a source tells me.

The special is expected to air just days before the Aug. 18 premiere.

As for the launch of the new season, the source also says a party is being planned for somewhere in Malibu. No word on who may perform. Mariah Carey and Usher have done the honors in the past.

So now let's hear from you. What are some of your favorite—or least favorite—moments from The Hills? Leave your answers below.
